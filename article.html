Top Master's Programs in New York: The Ultimate Guide 2025

5 min read By Team Go Masters

New York isn't just a city; it's a global classroom. For international students, it offers unparalleled access to Wall Street, the UN, and Silicon Alley.

Why Choose New York?

Home to Columbia, NYU, and Cornell Tech, NY offers high employability. 85% of graduates find jobs within 3 months.

Admission Checklist

  • GPA: Generally 3.0+ (varies by uni).
  • GRE: 310+ (Waived by some unis).
  • IELTS/TOEFL: 6.5 / 90+.

Budgeting

  • Tuition: $35k - $60k per year.
  • Living: $1,500 - $2,000 per month.
  • Scholarships: Merit-based aid available up to 50%.
